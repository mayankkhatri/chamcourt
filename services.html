Our Practice Areas

Comprehensive legal solutions tailored to your needs. We provide expert representation across a wide spectrum of legal disciplines.

Litigation Services

Civil Law

We handle a vast array of civil disputes with precision and care. From property disputes and breach of contract to tort claims and injunctions, our team ensures your rights are protected in civil courts. We strive for resolutions that align with your long-term interests.

Criminal Law

Facing criminal charges requires a robust and strategic defense. Our experienced criminal defense attorneys represent clients in all stages of criminal proceedings, from bail hearings to trials and appeals, ensuring a fair trial and vigorous defense of your liberty.

Family & Divorce Law

Navigating family disputes can be emotionally taxing. We provide compassionate and firm legal support for divorce, child custody, alimony, and domestic violence cases. Our goal is to resolve conflicts amicably where possible, while staunchly advocating for your interests.

Alternate Dispute Resolution

Litigation isn't always the only path. We specialize in Arbitration, Mediation, and Conciliation services to resolve disputes efficiently and cost-effectively outside the courtroom, preserving relationships and saving valuable time.

DRT Litigation

We represent banks, financial institutions, and borrowers in Debt Recovery Tribunals. Our expertise covers SARFAESI Act proceedings, debt recovery applications, and defense against unjust recovery measures, ensuring financial disputes are handled with legal acumen.

Negotiable Instrument Law

Specialized legal services for cases under Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act (Cheque Bounce cases). We assist in drafting notices, filing complaints, and defending clients to ensure swift legal recourse for dishonored financial instruments.

Intellectual Property Rights

Protect your innovation and creativity. We offer comprehensive services for Trademarks, Copyrights, and Patents, including registration, infringement litigation, and enforcement strategies to safeguard your intellectual assets in a competitive market.

Corporate Law

From company incorporation to mergers and acquisitions, we provide end-to-end corporate legal advisory. We handle shareholder disputes, NCLT matters, and corporate compliance, acting as a trusted partner for your business's legal health.

Revenue Law

Expert assistance in matters related to land revenue, property tax, and stamp duty. We represent clients before revenue authorities and tribunals, ensuring compliance and resolving disputes regarding land records and assessments.

Co-operative Society Law

We advise housing societies and co-operative banks on registration, bylaws, elections, and dispute resolution. Our team ensures smooth governance and legal compliance for co-operative bodies.

Service Matters

Representing employees and employers in disputes regarding employment, promotion, termination, and pension. We handle cases in Central Administrative Tribunals (CAT) and High Courts to protect employment rights.

Consumer Complaints

Fighting for consumer rights against unfair trade practices and defective goods/services. We represent clients in District, State, and National Consumer Disputes Redressal Commissions to secure compensation and justice.

Cyber Crime

In the digital age, cyber threats are real. We provide legal defense and prosecution support for cybercrimes including hacking, data theft, online fraud, and defamation, leveraging technical and legal expertise.

Commercial Courts

Handling high-value commercial disputes with speed and efficiency under the Commercial Courts Act. We deal with business contracts, trade disputes, and commercial arbitration enforcement.

Public Interest Litigation

Championing causes for the greater good. We file and contest PILs on issues of environmental protection, social justice, and governance, using the law as a tool for positive social change.

Quashing Petitions

Seeking relief from the High Court to quash baseless FIRs and criminal proceedings under Section 482 of the CrPC. We protect innocent individuals from harassment and abuse of the legal process.

CBI Matters

Specialized defense in high-profile cases investigated by the Central Bureau of Investigation. Our team navigates complex evidence and procedural intricacies to provide a formidable defense.

Economic Offences

Handling complex white-collar crimes including money laundering (PMLA), tax evasion, and corporate fraud. We provide strategic counsel to mitigate risks and defend against prosecution.

Arbitration

Expert representation in domestic and international arbitration proceedings. We assist in appointment of arbitrators, interim relief, and enforcement of arbitral awards.

Insolvency & Bankruptcy

Advising creditors and corporate debtors under the IBC. We assist in corporate insolvency resolution processes (CIRP), liquidation, and debt restructuring strategies.

Non-Litigation & Documentation

Document Registration

Seamless assistance with the drafting and registration of various legal documents, ensuring all statutory requirements are met for validity and enforceability.

Rent & Lease Agreements

Drafting robust Rent Agreements and Leave & License Agreements to protect the interests of both landlords and tenants, minimizing future disputes.

Partnership Deeds

Structuring and drafting comprehensive Partnership Deeds that clearly define roles, profit-sharing, and dispute resolution mechanisms for new ventures.

Contract Drafting

Tailored drafting of commercial contracts, service agreements, and MOUs. We ensure every clause is precise and legally sound to safeguard your business interests.

Legal Notices

Drafting and responding to legal notices with precision. A well-drafted notice often resolves disputes without the need for prolonged litigation.

Marriage Registration

Guiding couples through the process of marriage registration under various Marriage Acts, ensuring your union is legally recognized without hassle.

Adoption Deeds

Legal assistance in drafting and registering Adoption Deeds, ensuring the process complies with CARA guidelines and personal laws for a secure future.

Succession Certificates

Assisting legal heirs in obtaining Succession Certificates and Legal Heir Certificates to facilitate the smooth transfer of assets and debts.

Pedhi Namu

Expert services for obtaining Pedhi Namu (Family Tree Certificate), essential for inheritance and property mutation purposes.

Title Clearance

Conducting thorough due diligence and issuing Title Clearance Reports for real estate transactions to ensure the property is free from encumbrances.

Release of Mortgage

Facilitating the legal process for the Release of Mortgage upon repayment of loans, ensuring your property title is restored to its original state.
