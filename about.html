A Shared Vision

Chambers and Courtland was born from the shared ambition of two close friends, Ravi Singh Chhikara and Ashwani Kumar.

Guided by a relentless commitment to excellence and inspired by the blessings of Lord Shiva, we have steadily expanded our presence to become a trusted name for clients seeking robust legal representation. From the outset, our objective has been clear: to combine diligent preparation, strategic advocacy, and uncompromising integrity.

We believe that every matter entrusted to us deserves the utmost seriousness. We take pride in meeting timelines without ever compromising on quality, ensuring that our clients receive the best possible outcomes.

Cornerstones of Our Practice

We measure our success by that of our clients, working tirelessly to safeguard their rights and advance their interests.

Uncompromising Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards. Transparency is central to our practice; we ensure clients remain fully informed with accessible updates at every stage.

Strategic Advocacy

Our approach combines deep legal knowledge with tactical foresight. We prepare diligently to deliver robust representation in the courts of Delhi-NCR and Haryana.

Global Ambition

Though a young firm, we are laying the foundation for an international footprint. Our vision is to be recognized globally for litigation and advisory excellence.

Diverse Expertise

Client-Centric Focus

Result-Oriented

We are driven by results. Our team tirelessly explores every legal avenue to secure the best possible outcome, ensuring your interests are paramount.

Looking to the Future
